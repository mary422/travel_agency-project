About Us

Travelo was registered back in 2007 being the first travel agency in the central region. Initially, it started its operations with travelling operations only before introducing other services in 2013. It was licenced by Tourism Regulatory Authority(TRA) in 2014 being among the first travel agencies to be registered in Kenya. The company is run by a CEO and two directors with over 20,000 members. It has an asset base of about Kes 5.9Billion.

Our vision

To be the leading agency in offering viable travelling services worldwide to all our customers for a sustainable economic and social growth.

Our Mission

Exploring adventorous sites, places, historical places, mountain hiking and other services.

Why Us

We offer the best customer travelling services that ensure satisfaction to our members and acts as a watchtower of the sustainability, growth and development of our comapany.
